# OSTicket - Post Installation Configuration
This tutorial outlines the post-installation configuration of the open-source help desk ticketing system osTicket.

<h2>Environments and Technologies Used</h2>

- Microsoft Azure (Virtual Machine/Compute)
- Remote Desktop
- Open-source Ticketing System osTicket

<h2>Operating Systems Used</h2>

- Windows 10

<h1>Logging in to osTicket</h1>

<p>
Log in to by going to http://localhost/osTicket/scp/login.php with the Admin user you created while installing osTicket.
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/E99byc0.jpeg" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<h1>Creating an Admin user</h1>

<p>
Click on the "Admin Panel".
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/3Ian2Ku.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<p>
Click on the "Agents", click on "Roles", then click on "Add New Role".
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/maYFEWA.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<p>
Give the new role an appropriate name, then go to permissions and click every box to give it the permissions that it needs. Click "Add Role".
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/KHFRQQy.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/1p7FDXs.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<h1>Creating the Admin Department</h1>

<p>
Navigate over to "Departments" and click "Add New Department".
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/PD0ilhv.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<p>
Give your new department a name and click "Create Dept".
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/b1pxVFw.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<h1>Creating Teams</h1>

<p>
Navigate over to "Teams" and click "Add New Team".
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/VcXXH6h.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<p>
Give your new team a name and click "Create team".
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/WitIaKC.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<p>
Do these steps again to create another support team with a higher level.
<p>
<br />

<h1>Ticket Permissions</h1>

<p>
Click "Admin Panel", Click "Settings", then click "User Settings".
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/RZi0VDH.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<h1>Creating Working Agents For Your Team</h1>

<p>
Go to "Admin Panel", Click "Agents", then click "Add New Agent". Fill out the info you need and assign them to a team. Repeat as many times as necessary.
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/SvdJF4w.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<h1>Creating Customers</h1>

<p>
Go to "Agent Panel", Click "Users", then click "Add New User". Fill out the info you need and assign them to a team. Repeat as many times as necessary.
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/L2eoorV.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<h1>Creating SLA</h1>

<p>
Go to "Admin Panel", Click "Manage", click "SLA", then click "Add New SLA Plan". Give it an appropriate name, grace period, and schedule. Repeat as many times as necessary.
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/SvdJF4w.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
<br />

<h1>Creating Help Topics</h1>

<p>
Go to "Admin Panel", Click "Manage", click "Help Topics", then click "Add New Help Topic". Give it an appropriate name, grace period, and schedule. Repeat as many times as necessary.
<p>
<img src="https://i.imgur.com/A5VNi1f.png" height="80%" width="80%" alt="Disk Sanitization Steps"/>
</p>
